Current Projects

2015 Camera Gimbal for UAV

I am currently working with a subteam to design a new camera gimbal for the Fenrir 3 unmanned aerial vehicle being build by the NC State Aerial Robotics Club. The new design should improve on the last version by reducing overall width to accommodate a wiring harness on each side and balancing a different camera setup. The gimbal is gyroscopically controlled to stay oriented directly at the ground for target acquisition.
